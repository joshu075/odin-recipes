Asparagus and Eggs

Description

Asparagus and eggs make the best spring breakfast or a beautiful light lunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 8 asparagus stalks, trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Heat skillet and add olive oil and garlic; cook until garlic is fragrant.
  2. Add asparagus and cook until fork-tender.
  3. Spread asparagus out and crack the eggs over the asparagus.
  4. Cover with lid and cook until egg whites and yolk are done to desired consistency.
