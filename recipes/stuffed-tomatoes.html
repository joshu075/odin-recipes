Air-Fried Italian Stuffed Tomatoes

Description

Garlic, parmesan cheese, fresh basil, and toasted nuts turn brown rice into a superstar stuffing in this Italian-inspired tomato side dish that's cooked in the air fryer.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium tomatoes
  • olive oil, as needed
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 1/3 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped toasted walnuts
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, divided, or more to taste
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup Italian-seasoned bread crumbs, divided
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut tops off tomatoes and scoop out flesh with a melon baller.
  2. Brush the bottom of the air fryer basket with olive oil.
  3. Combine cooked rice, parmesan cheese, goat cheese, walnuts, basil, and garlic in a medium bowl.
  4. Fill tomatoes with mixture and place them in air fryer.
  5. Cook at 188 degrees C until tomatoes are tender.
